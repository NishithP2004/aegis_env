            Reward function (how step rewards are computed)
          
          

            

              Rewards are dense along the pipeline, then a
              final payout on the last step. Each episode starts with a
              flow_bank of 0.10. Intermediate
              transitions subtract small amounts from the bank as “progress” rewards.
            

            

              
Pipeline transitions

              
    
                
  • 
                  arbiter → scrutinizer,
                  scrutinizer → validator: reward
                  0.02 each (bank decreases).
                
    • 
                
  • 
                  validator with
                  routing_decision = proceed → mentor: reward
                  0.02.
                
    • 
                
  • 
                  validator with
                  revise → scrutinizer (refinement loop): reward
                  0.01. At most two refinement loops; exceeding that or an
                  invalid route ends the episode with a fatal step (reward 0).
                
    • 
              

            

            

              
Final step (mentor)

              

                The episode completes at mentor. The step reward is the sum of:
              

              
    
                
  • 
                  Accuracy (up to 0.6): compare
                  normalized proposed score vs. hidden human score:
                  0.6 × (1 − |norm_agent − norm_human|) when
                  proposed_score ∈ [0, max_score].
                
    • 
                
  • 
                  Validity (up to 0.3): if
                  agent_reasoning has at least 10 words,
                  0.3 × Jaccard(reasoning, reference_feedback).
                
    • 
                
  • 
                  Flow bank remainder: whatever is left in
                  flow_bank is paid out with the final step.
                
    • 
              

              

                The total is clamped to [0, 1]. When the episode ends, diagnostics
                include accuracy_reward,
                validity_reward,
                flow_bank_payout, and
                total_step_reward.
